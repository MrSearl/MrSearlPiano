Motown Music

Task 2 - ‘War by Edwin Starr

War is a soul song written by Norman Whitfield and Barrett Strong for Motown Records in 1969. Although it was first recorded by The Temptations, the best-known version was performed by Edwin Starr.

The song became famous for its powerful message against war and its energetic vocal performance. Edwin Starr's strong voice helped turn the song into a protest anthem.

Like many Motown songs, War uses a call-and-response style, which has its roots in Gospel music. The memorable lyrics, driving rhythm and repeated phrases encouraged listeners to join in and helped spread the song's message of peace.
