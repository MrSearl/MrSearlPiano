Motown Music

Task 1 - ‘My Girl’ by The Temptations

The Temptations were an American vocal group who became famous through Motown Records during the 1960s and 1970s. They were known for their stylish clothes, impressive dance moves and smooth vocal harmonies.

The group had many hit songs and became one of the most successful soul and R&B groups of all time. Their music influenced many artists who came after them.

The Temptations were especially known for their rich harmonies and the way different singers shared the melody and backing parts. Their performances helped shape the sound of soul music and made them one of the most important vocal groups in popular music history.
