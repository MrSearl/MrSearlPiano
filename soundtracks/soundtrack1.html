Task 1 - ‘The X Files’ by Mark Snow

Mark Snow (born August 26, 1946) is an American composer for film and television. Among his most famous compositions is the theme music for sci-fi television series The X-Files. The theme reached Number Two in the UK Singles Chart. This was an American science fiction horror drama television series. It is part of The X-Files franchise, created by Chris Carter. The series recounted the exploits of FBI Special Agents Fox Mulder (David Duchovny) and Dana Scully (Gillian Anderson) who investigate X-Files.
