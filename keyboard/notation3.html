Task 3 - ‘Clair de Lune’ by Claude Debussy

‘Suite bergamasque’ is one of the most famous piano suites by Claude Debussy. It was first composed by Debussy around 1890, at the age of 28, but was significantly revised just before its publication in 1905. The third and most famous movement of Suite bergamasque is "Clair de lune", in Dflat major. Its name comes from Verlaine's poem "Clair de lune", which means "moonlight" in French.
