Task 4 - ‘Tiger Rag' by Art Tatum

Art Tatum was an American jazz pianist who grew up in Toledo, Ohio. He began playing professionally at a young age and even had his own radio programme while still a teenager.

In 1932, he moved away from Toledo and performed in clubs in cities such as New York, Chicago and Los Angeles. He was known for his exciting and creative performances.

Tatum's piano playing was incredibly fast and technically demanding. He used complex harmonies, rapid scales and improvised melodies to create his unique style.

His performances often featured changes in tempo and dynamics, adding expression and energy. Many musicians considered him one of the greatest jazz pianists of all time, and even highly trained classical pianists found his music difficult to copy.
