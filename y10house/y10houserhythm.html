Rhythm - House Music

Rhythm is one of the most important features of house music. House tracks are almost always written in 4/4 time and use a steady pulse that makes the music easy to dance to. A common feature is the “four-on-the-floor” beat, where the kick drum plays on every beat of the bar. Syncopated hi-hats, percussion patterns, and basslines are often layered above the drum groove to create movement and energy. Repetition is also important, helping to maintain a consistent groove throughout the track.
