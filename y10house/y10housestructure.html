Structure - House Music

Structure in house music is usually repetitive and based on the gradual introduction and removal of musical layers. House tracks commonly include sections such as introductions, breakdowns, build-ups, drops, and outros. Repeated loops and grooves help maintain the energy of the music, while changes in texture and instrumentation create contrast between sections. Many house tracks are designed for DJs, with extended introductions and endings making transitions between songs easier.
